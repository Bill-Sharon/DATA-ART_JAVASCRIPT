Formula 1: 1950 - 2025

1950 - The Beginning

First F1 World Championship season. Giuseppe Farina wins in an Alfa Romeo.

1967 - Safety Innovations

Introduction of full-face helmets and improved crash barriers.

1988 - McLaren Dominance

Senna & Prost win 15/16 races for McLaren-Honda.

1994 - Tragedy & Safety Reforms

Death of Ayrton Senna prompts major safety overhauls.

2021 - Verstappen's First Title

Max Verstappen wins after a dramatic Abu Dhabi finale.

2025 - New Era

Cutting-edge hybrid and aero technology define the 2025 season.
